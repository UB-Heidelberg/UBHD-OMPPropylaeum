“Studies in African Archaeology” (SAA) is a publication series published since 1984 by the Archaeological Museum in Poznań. In the series appear monographs on the prehistory of north-eastern Africa and proceedings of quadrennial international conferences entitled “Later Prehistory of Northeastern Africa” (LPNEA) that have been organised in Poland since 1980.
